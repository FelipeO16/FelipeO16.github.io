About
Hello, I'm Felipe Oliveira, a passionate Full-Stack developer in the world of software development. With 3 years of experience in Backend and 2 years in Front-end, I'm always seeking to enhance my skills and knowledge in the field.
Currently, I'm studying Computer Science to stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work on challenging projects and collaborate with talented teams.
I have strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue.js, Node.js, LUA, MySQL, and other relevant technologies. Additionally, I'm passionate about JQuery, Atomic Design, and SQLite, which allows me to create innovative and scalable solutions. I believe in the power of teamwork and the importance of clear and effective communication to achieve exceptional results.
I always strive to deliver high-quality and efficient solutions, with a focus on usability and user experience. In my portfolio, you'll find examples of the projects I've worked on, showcasing my ability to develop high-quality applications.
I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my experience and knowledge. If you're looking for a passionate and highly skilled Full-Stack developer, I'm ready to take on new challenges and help turn your ideas into reality. Let's work together to achieve exceptional results and drive the success of your projects.
